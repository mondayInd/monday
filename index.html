About Us!

Monday Business Solutions is a startup providing SAAS and logistic as services to connect departmental stores to nearby wholesalers, distributors, industries and enable them to conduct trade virtually.

Virtual trade is going to drastically reduce the crowd at the wholesale market, creating a safer business environment for all parties involved, in this dire time of COVID-19 pandemic, lesser the crowd, safer we all are. The Stratup has been operating since March-2019 and was incorporated in July-2018. Monday is the first mover in this sector in the entire of East-India and the company's "Smart Logistic Solution" is a segment first in the hyperlocal B2B logistic industry. Monday is also the first startup of its kind to server business across remote rural regions. The hyperlocal B2B logistics industry and the wholesale/distributor to retail supply chain being unorganised is susceptible to the COVID-19 pandemic and organising the sector would contribute immensely towards the prevention of the disease. The startup constitues of innovation driven heads. Creating something valuable, that has never been created before is what drives the team.
