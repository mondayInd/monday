About Us

What is Monday?

Monday is a logistics company that not only deliver goods but empowers alongwith.


Why does Monday Exist?

Monday enables rural businesses buy at cheaper rates and sell their respective indigenous/authentic products at a better price.


How do we do it?

We do it by connecting the remote rural market to the global market with the help of tolls like cutting edge technology and smart logistics.


When & Where?

Monday was found on July 2018 in the small town of Karimganj, Assam, India.


MISSION:

Our Mision is to empower the remote rural population economically.


VISION:

Monday is a rural India based startup which aims at taking the wholesale industry of the doorsteps of the Rural Retail store and taking the authentic products from the Rural Market and selling them at the global market.
